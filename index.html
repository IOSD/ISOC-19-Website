About IOSD Summer Of Code

At International Orgnanisation of Software Developers, most of us come from a vibrant Open Source Community, and that makes us strongly believe in free and open source software.

Each year we guide thousands of students on their journey in software developement and community building. Over the past four years, we have guided more than thosands of students gain expertise in Android, Web, Data Science and analytics. To be able to stay connected with our alumni, and reach out the wider community of budding software developers, we launched an annual summer programme - OSSI which stands for Open Source Summer Initiative.

We took inspiration from other such open source summer programmes like Google Summer of Code, Google Code-In, Summer of KDE, RGSoC and many others to start our own programme to engage all of you via IOSD Summer Of Code.

In IOSD Summer Of Code 2019, we are continuing where we left off, the same challenge, in a whole new avatar, with bigger projects and with a more determined and upright resolution to celebrate diversity among our participants, IOSD Summer Of code 2019 has come up with a unique concept acknowledging and appreciating the efforts of our girl students. The best performing girl will be recognized with special prizes and goodies.

We are organising special introductory sessions that can be streamed over our twitch channel @iosdofficial as well as students can visit our Adarsh Nagar Campus for the same.
