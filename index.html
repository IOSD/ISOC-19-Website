About IOSD

“For good ideas and true innovation, you need human interaction, conflict, argument, debate.

Our goal is to provide a platform for developers to come together discuss ideas and create projects in an adjuvant environment. IOSD founding principle was to promote technological innovation among college students. To implement this we conduct events such as mock hackathons, discussion forums, monthly competitions, seminars and workshops all around the year to get students more in touch with the latest technological trends and to provide an industrial environment.

About IOSD Summer Of Code

At International Organisation of Software Developers, most of us come from a vibrant Open Source Community, and that makes us strongly believe in free and open source software.

Each year we guide thousands of students on their journey in software developement and community building. Over the past four years, we have guided more than thousands of students gain expertise in Android, Web, Data Science and analytics. To be able to stay connected with our alumni, and reach out the wider community of budding software developers, we launched an annual summer programme - OSSI which stands for Open Source Summer Initiative.

We took inspiration from other such open source summer programmes like Google Summer of Code, Google Code-In, Summer of KDE, RGSoC and many others to start our own programme to engage all of you via IOSD Summer Of Code.

In IOSD Summer Of Code 2019, we are continuing where we left off, the same challenge, in a whole new avatar, with bigger projects and with a more determined and upright resolution to celebrate diversity among our participants, IOSD Summer Of code 2019 has come up with a unique concept acknowledging and appreciating the efforts of our girl students. The best performing girl will be recognized with special prizes and goodies.

Code It

Task is to design an Online Coding Test App with IDE for famous programming languages like Python, C++, C, Java. This will be just like Codechef, but with fewer functionalities. The user will login using pre-provided credentials, complete the test and then the score will be recorded in database for Test Admins. Test Admins will have ability to make a test and send invitations for the same.


Skills Required : Python, Java, SQL, Android ADK, GIT, Working with Backend skills.
